Kimre competes in the corrosive specialty tier where application depth and ETO capability command a 20–40% price premium over catalog alternatives. Volume is deliberately limited to protect margins and service quality.
Strategic Position
"Specialty High-End, Not Volume Catalog"
  • 〈 Application depth for harsh chemistries — Kimre engineers for H₂SO₄, HNO₃, HCl, and mixed-acid environments where off-the-shelf products fail. This expertise is not replicable via catalog.
  • 〈 Custom ETO for non-standard vessel IDs — Engineered-to-Order capacity serves projects that catalog competitors simply decline. Kimre wins RFQs by saying yes when others say "outside standard."
  • 〈 50+ years of fiber bed IP — B-GON® proprietary fiber technology and decades of application data create a moat that cannot be bridged by new entrants or catalog expansion alone.
